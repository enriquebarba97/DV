World Cup Data Visualization

Welcome to World Cup Data Visualization!

In this World Cup Data Visualization page, we've prepared three plots for you. You can find a world map on the left, in which orange represents countries that have participated in the World Cup (Grey means no participation). You can compare the number of score each team made on a 15 minutes interval, and you can compare up to three teams. In the third plot, you can find the strategy each team each year used, and find the association between strategies and winning rate / number of goals. We also show the year of the team joining the World Cup. Have fun!
